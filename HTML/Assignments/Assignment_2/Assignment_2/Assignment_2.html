Sir Isaac Newton

Sir Isaac Newton was an English mathematician, physicist, astronomer, and author who is widely recognized as one of the greatest mathematicians and most influential scientists of all time. His work laid the foundation for classical mechanics and modern physics.

  • Formulated the laws of motion and universal gravitation
  • Invented calculus independently (alongside Leibniz)
  • Made groundbreaking discoveries in optics
  • Published the Principia Mathematica in 1687
